Got Questions?

How do I choose the right support plan for my business?

We offer three main support plans – Basic, Standard, and Premium. Each plan is designed to meet different levels of IT support needs. The Basic plan is ideal for small businesses with basic IT needs, the Standard plan is suited for businesses requiring more advanced support, and the Premium plan offers comprehensive support for businesses with critical IT needs.

How does remote assistance work?

Remote assistance allows us to securely access your systems over the internet to troubleshoot and resolve issues. This enables us to provide hands-on support without the need for an on-site visit, saving you time and minimizing disruptions.

How do I get started with your services?

To get started, you can contact us through the contact form on our website. We'll discuss your needs and help you choose the best support plan or service for your business.

What happens if I exceed my monthly support hours?

If you exceed your monthly support hours, additional support will be billed at a predefined hourly rate. We will always inform you in advance if you are approaching your limit to avoid any surprises.

What are your support hours?

TechSavyPro is a US-based company and is available for support between 8am - 8pm ET on weekdays. Weekend support is available by scheduling in advance with our team.

Which countries do you offer support to?

Currently, we offer support to businesses in the US and Canada. We plan to add more international support in the future.
